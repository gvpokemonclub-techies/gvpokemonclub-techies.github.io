Hello there! Welcome to the world of POKEMON! We're the GVSU Pokemon Club, and are a community for those who enjoy anything Pokémon - video/mobile games, TCG, shows, merch, and more. All fans are welcome!

↓ Open Pokedex to See Past Events! ↓

Sharpedo Tank

Teams of fish will enter the tank, and draw Pokémon based on prompts given by the sharpedos! Present your invention to the sharpedos (judges), and whoever has the best one WINS. The team that wins become the new sharpedos, and the previous sharpedos replace the team.

Draw From Memory

Teams will all be given a pokemon to draw from memory on a whiteboard. The team with the most accurate drawing wins! Have fun!

Pokemon GO Walks

We sometimes go for Pokemon GO walks when the weather is nice, and we have a thriving gvsu pokemon go playerbase to connect with! We also host an annual trip to the Meijer Gardens, where they have many poke-stops!

Valentines Day Pokemon Cards

Show your love by making custom pokemon card valentines letters! Whether you make them rizzy or funny, it's good time!

St.Patricks Day Shiny Hunting

Bring a Pokemon game of your choosing, pick a Pokemon to hunt, and try your best! We’ll also have a Pokemon movie playing, and may provide snacks!

Pokemon Trivia

Show us the extent of your Pokémon knowledge in Jeopardy, Kahoots, and more!
